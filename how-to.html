How to use ESL Report Writer

Quick start in two minutes

  1. Open the builder. Launch the Report Writer and pick the output language and tone.
  2. Complete the student profile. Add the student name, gender, perspective, trimester, and report register.
  3. Capture performance data. Score each area on the 0-10 scale, then note traits, highlights, and priorities.
  4. Generate content. Use Generate Full Report and optionally Get Improvement Ideas for actionable next steps.
  5. Refine and deliver. Tweak the copy with Edit with AI, then copy it into your reporting platform.

Choose the layout that suits your workflow

The default step-by-step mode guides you through three clear stages: gathering context, rating performance, and generating outputs. Navigation indicates whether you are on Step 1, 2, or 3.

Single-page mode displays everything at once. It is ideal for quick data entry when you already know the student details and want to move fast.

Key fields at a glance

  • Language sets the language for headings and body copy.
  • Report tone adjusts the voice: neutral, friendly, empathetic, concise, or growth-focused.
  • Perspective swaps between third person ("she") and second person ("you").
  • Ratings (0-10) influence the balance of praise and development points.
  • Character traits highlight personality cues you want to surface.
  • Areas to improve drive the improvement strategies the AI suggests.
  • Other points lets you add bespoke context or classroom anecdotes.
  • Salutation controls the closing sentiment for the report.

Generate, refine, deliver

Create the draft

Run Generate Full Report after completing the required fields to produce a tailored narrative.

Plan next steps

Select Get Improvement Ideas to transform low-scoring areas into practical actions.

Polish and share

Fine-tune tone, emphasis, or length in Edit with AI, then copy the ready-to-send version.

Troubleshooting

  • No report appears: double-check required fields (name, language, tone, trimester) and try again.
  • Edits are not applied: give precise guidance such as "shorten by 20%" or "more encouraging tone", then regenerate if needed.
